Whether or not I have sent this to you as my portfolio or you have just stumbled upon this page-- hi there. I'm Jadden Picardal, and welcome to my small HTML corner on the internet! I'm currently a Computer Engineering student at Purdue with a passion for design and motion graphics, and I looooove building bridges-- between idea and implementation, engineering and art, and people and the technology that shapes their lives.

Throughout my several projects, I've found joy in blending technical precision with creativity, but the most rewarding part has always been seeing how these creations impact others-- how they teach, inspire, or simply entertain.

Anyway, this website is meant to be raw and expressive while easily displaying my work, representing not just what I create, but who I am. I want this site to serve as more than a portfolio; I want it to be a space where I share my ideas and reflect on my growth.

Thanks for stopping by :)

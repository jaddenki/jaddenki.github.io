five dynamics trailer process

page in progress*

i was given a week to make a trailer for 5d, and here is the full process, from the planning to the final execution :D

purpose

five dynamics is a new suborbital rocket club @ purdue. the goal of this video was to look flashy and disruptive, to attract people to the organization & make them wonder "woah. what is this.. it's so cool...".

this task was a bit different than my previous commissions since people would usually come to me with a specific vision in mind-- i, the motion designer, am supposed to help envision my client's style. in this case, i was the one who built the brand from the ground up.

this was a really fun project in the sense that 1. i created the branding & style for 5d, so the final stylistic choices were up to me, and 2. i had my friends in 5d to bounce ideas off of!

audio

my friend gave me the song whiplash by le sserafim as 'vibe inspo', so i started by looking for the right audio. thankfully, i stumbled upon flint - man in the beast, and thought it was the perfect audio to use.
